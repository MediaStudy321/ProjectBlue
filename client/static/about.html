The World

A Rogue lite turn based RPG. A dungeon crawler game whose combat is revolved around dice rolls.

Progression

Character Development

Player starts out with a selection of 3 character classes. Level up the character by completing quest and dungeon runs. Leveling up characters unlocks new skills and strengthen character. Upon reaching max level, players are offer the chance to change thier class into a more advance class. Will reset the character to level 1 up in return, gain access to stronger skills.

Adventure Ranks

Leveling up Adventure ranks allow more access to shop. Level up adventure ranks by taking on guild commission and running through the dungeon. With higher adventure ranks allows for class change quest and much better equipment that can be brought from shop. Also unlock access to other crafts such as blacksmithing and potion brewing.

Dungeon Completion

The goal of the game is to complete all floors of the dungeon in 1 run. Players are able to see their records and how far they have made it down. There are a total of 20 floors in the dungeon and a boss harboring every 5th floor. The difficulty increases after every floor and so will the quaility of rewards.

Unique

Dungeon Rooms

Upon entering the dungeon, players must find a room with a stair case in order to advance onto the next floor. The room with the stair will be completely ramdomized, it would even be in the first tile when spawned in. Each room has up to 5 events that can be encountered once, either there are mobs, treasure chest (or a mimic), events that buffs or debuffs the party, traps, or an empty room. Reentering the room would not trigger the event again.

Dice

In combat, only dices are usd to roll to attack as well as initate. The dice themselves are unique in a sense that it can have repeating numbers in them. They can have sides up to 6, 8, 10, 12, etc.

Party System

To enter a dungeon, there must be a max of 4 characters in the party. If the user cannot find actual people to pair up with, they can spend money to higher mercanaries to tag along. The Merc system has 2 steps to it, advertisment and hiring. Both cost seperatly. The more money invested into advertisment, the more stronger the option of npc. The Mercs are scaled towards the adventure rank of the player. Stronger Mercs have their stats more optimized towards thier class while weaker ones, although at the same level, will be missing some skills as well as bad equipments. Players are free to provid equipments but if they party fails to leave safely, the equipment would be loss. In the event where the players are not able to hire the mercs, the guild would always offer free npcs to join but they would be a few levels under as well as having only basic equipments.

Goal

The goal of the game is clear the final floor of the dungeon.
